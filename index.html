Paris - The City of Lights

Welcome to Paris!

Paris, the capital of France, is known for its rich history, beautiful architecture, and delicious cuisine. It is one of the most visited cities in the world.

Top Attractions

Paris is home to many world-famous landmarks and museums.

The Eiffel Tower

One of the most iconic structures in the world, the Eiffel Tower offers stunning views of the city.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre is the largest art museum in the world and is home to the famous Mona Lisa painting.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

This stunning Gothic cathedral is a must-visit for history and architecture lovers.

French Cuisine

Paris is famous for its delicious food, including croissants, escargot, and crème brûlée.
